where do i see myself in 10 years

  • Technical Expertise: By then, you'll likely have become an even more seasoned developer. You may have gained expertise in various programming languages, frameworks, and technologies. Staying up-to-date with the latest trends will be crucial to remain competitive.
  • Leadership and Management: You might have transitioned into a leadership or management role. This could involve leading development teams, managing projects, or even starting your own web development company.
